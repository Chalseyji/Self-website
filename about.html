Our Services

  • Welcome to Shaunak Films, a self-made social media platform founded by Shaunak, a passionate individual with a keen interest in photography, videography, editing, and cinematography. With over two years of dedicated experience in the field, Shaunak embarked on this journey fueled by a vision to bring dreams to life on a budget. Shaunak Films prides itself on being a budget-friendly film company, where clients can see their creative visions come to fruition without breaking the bank. Despite being a one-man team, Shaunak handles every aspect of the filmmaking process, from conceptualization to execution, ensuring personalized attention to each project. Our services encompass photography, videography, editing, and cinematography, catering to a diverse range of creative needs. Shaunak's talent and dedication have earned recognition, with notable achievements including winning the bronze prize at IFP 2023 for the film "Love Stop. Late Night" and securing the second place at KIIT University for his contributions to the film society. At Shaunak Films, our philosophy revolves around democratizing filmmaking, making it accessible to everyone regardless of budget constraints. We believe in harnessing creativity and innovation to deliver high-quality productions that resonate with audiences. As a testament to our commitment to excellence, we collaborate with institutions like KIIT University's film society, receiving support and access to equipment to enhance our production capabilities. Our ultimate goal is to become the go-to destination for exceptional photography, videography, editing, and cinematography services, making the magic of filmmaking accessible to all. Join us at Shaunak Films as we embark on a journey to make budget-friendly filmmaking a reality for everyone.
